The figure highlights those countries whose carbon emissions per capita in a year (those highlighed in orange) are less than that of a person taking one flight between the two specified cities.

In addition, the darker the country is, the more the amount of carbon footprint per capita is. You can hover your mouse over the country to see the country name and their CO2eq per capita.
